Frequently Asked Questions
How does it work?

Let me ask you this - Where do you find new people to follow? The explore page. There you find new content and new accounts to follow. By following celebrities who post similar content to yours, Instagram's explore algorithm shares your content to other users. For example, if you follow a bunch of cooking celebrities, Instagram knows you will have things in common with other people who follow the same people so it shares your posts to their explore page. The constant following and unfollowing cycle renews this and constantly broadcasts your posts to the maximum audience to get you real followers, likes and user engagement!

How many followers can I expect?

We have found that you get approximately 1 new follower for every 10 new people you follow.

How does the liking bot work?

When trying to grow an Instagram page, what's the best way for you to get new followers? You get involved. You follow other people and like their posts! They see you and many times follow you back! That's all there is to it, except our bot does all the work for you, 24 hours a day around the clock, subscribing to and liking up to 1000 people and posts a day.

How fast should I run it?

With the unlimited and desktop versions you can go up to 60 actions an hour but Instagram will block you for 24 hours if you go over 60 in an hour! I reccomend limiting it to 50 so you can still follow and unfollow who you want when your'e browsing Instagram but 60 an hour is the limit!

What are the limits?

Instagram only allows you to do 60 "Actions" per minute. "Actions" can be either "Follow" or "Unfollow." For example, in a single hour, you could legally do 40 Follows and 20 Unfollows total. Any more than that would trigger an alarm on their end that your'e going too fast. But stay under that and they'll never be upset! "Likes" run on a separate counter and you can do up to 60 in an hour. Note the free version can only perform up to 20 total actions and 20 total likes an hour.
